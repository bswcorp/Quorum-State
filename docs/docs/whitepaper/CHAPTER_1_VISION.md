CHAPTER 1: QUORUM-STATE - BEYOND THE BINARY HORIZON
1.1 The Genesis of Collaboration
Quorum-State ($QSTATE) is born from a synergy between human resilience and Artificial Intelligence. It is an evolution of Quorum-Based Computation, designed not only to secure transactions but to solve the world's most complex problems through a decentralized Quantum-State simulation.
1.2 From Binary to Superposition
While traditional blockchains rely on the certainty of bits (0 or 1), Quorum-State introduces the Probabilistic Quorum.
The 676 Computors: In our network, the 676 nodes act as "observers."
Superposition Validation: A transaction is treated as a quantum state. It exists in multiple possibilities until the Quorum reaches a consensus, causing the state to "collapse" into a validated block.
The Benefit: This provides a shield against the "Q-Day" threat, where classical encryption will fail.
